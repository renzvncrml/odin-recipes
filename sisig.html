Sisig

4.3 / 5

Prep: 12mins | Cook: 1hr 30mins

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from pork jowl and ears, pork belly, and chicken liver, which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers. It originates from the Pampanga region in Luzon. Sisig is a staple of Kapampangan cuisine

Ingredients

Steps

  • 500 grams ppork belly sliced about 1/2 inch thick
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 red onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 lemon or 3-4 calamansi juice
  • 1-2 chili peppers minced
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • egg
  1. Season pork belly slices with salt and pepper. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill pork belly until well done. Pork skin should be crispy.
  3. Once grilled, chop the pork belly into small fine pieces.
  4. In a bowl, combine the chopped meat, onions, lemon juice and chili pepper. Season with soy sauce and pepper.
  5. Warm it in a pan with a bit of oil if desired. Alternatively, heat a sizzling plate and add a some butter or margarine. Add the Sisig and top with egg.
  6. Serve hot with rice or beer
